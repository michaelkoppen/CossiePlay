Goals

The website needs to make more people interested in cosplay and therefore increase sales for CossiePlay. Cossieplay also wants to broaden the age range for cosplay and get more young people involved.

Success Evaluation

The websites success will be judged by CossiePlay's sale growth and increases in party turnouts.

Target Audience

CossiePlay's current target audience consists of locals in the average age range of 35-50. The website needs to expand the target audience for younger people such as high school students and young adults.
